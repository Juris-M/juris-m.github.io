Legal Resource Registry

The Legal Resource Registry contains an expanding collection of machine-readable mappings of legal jurisdictions and the courts that serve them. The repository contains scripts used to produce both the condensed maps that drive the Jurisdiction field of legal items in Jurism, and the jurisdiction-specific abbreviations used to produce correct legal citations.
